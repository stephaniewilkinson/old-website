About

Hi! I'm Stephanie. I'm a web developer with four years of experience with Ruby and frameworks like Roda and Rails, and have leveled up my Javascript skills at General Assembly for the MEAN stack. I am the Technology Director at the California Association of Independent Schools.

I live in LA and have been working since 2009 with schools and the nonprofit sector, and I love to give back to my community by teaching RailsGirls and other volunteering. I'm active in the LA programmer community and run a weekly programming meetup called Ruby in Hollywood. I also have a lot of side projects in various languages and frameworks. Right now I'm working on a Phoenix app using Phoenix channels.

I built and manage a Rails app for the California Association of Independent Schools, and travel the state giving talks and workshops to teach schools how to use the app.

Languages: JavaScript, Ruby, HTML, CSS, Python

Libraries/Frameworks: jQuery, Node.js, Mongoose, Express.js, AngularJS, Rails, Roda

Databases: Mongo & PostgreSQL

Related Skills: Agile, Git, Project Management, Pivotal Tracker, Vim, Teaching

Work

Bookwyrm is a Roda app for bookworms. It copies books from your Goodreads shelves and imports them to your Bookmooch wishlist.

The Accreditation Portal is a Rails app for California schools being accredited with the California Association of Independent Schools.

Concert Match is a MEAN stack app that you can use by logging in with Spotify and it will show you concerts of your favorite bands coming up near your location.

Curl Queen Doreen is a single page HTML site, featuring Bootstrap, responsive buttons, pages, and assets, and integration with Yelp and Instagram APIs.

Master Your Macros is a Rails app for managing your food intake according to macronutrients.

A playable, in-browser version of the strategy game Abalone.

Abalone is a challenging game of strategy that for two players. Each take turns moving marbles around the board, with the objective to remove other player's marbles.

Deployed on firebase for multi-player enjoyment using firebase dB. You can play with anyone online if you both visit the hosted version on firebase, since it has a firebase dB backend.
