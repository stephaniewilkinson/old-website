About

Hi! I'm Stephanie. I live in LA and have been working since 2009 with schools and the nonprofit sector. I love to give back to my community by running a meetup, teaching RailsGirls and other volunteering.

I'm active in the LA programmer community and run a weekly programming meetup called Ruby in Hollywood. I also have a lot of side projects in various languages and frameworks, such as Ruby on Rails, and MEAN stack. Right now I'm working on an Elixir/Phoenix app using channels.

A playable, in-browser version of the strategy game Abalone.

Abalone is a challenging game of strategy that for two players. Each take turns moving marbles around the board, with the objective to remove other player's marbles.

Deployed on firebase for multi-player enjoyment using firebase dB. You can play with anyone online if you both visit the hosted version on firebase, since it has a firebase dB backend.
